Design Process

As a senior product designer, I’ve worked on three main areas: building products from scratch, enhancing features, and redesigning products to improve user experience and meet business goals.

Understanding the problem

I begin by identifying the problem’s what, why, where, and when to understand its root cause. Then, I develop strategies, including design approaches, identifying opportunities, addressing business gaps, and considering user mental models, to create an effective PRD.

Proposing the solution

When proposing solutions, I create design iterations that include information architecture, user flows, user personas, and clearly defined user goals. These iterations cover unique screens, error scenarios, edge cases, and prototypes.

Working with developers

After the design approval, I create a detailed design handoff page that includes all screens, error states, edge cases, and accessibility guidelines using ARIA labels. Once development is complete, I do the design quality check to ensure everything aligns with the designs.
