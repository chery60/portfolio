Educator platform

Redesinging the assign flow in assignments module

Introduction

The assignments module in Toddle‚Äôs educator platform is one of the most frequently used. It lets teachers assign tasks, learning experiences, worksheets, and assessments. Currently, users can assign tasks, set submission deadlines, schedule when the assignment becomes visible to students, and close submissions after the due date. Teachers can also set specific times for these actions.

Identification of Problem


Inflexible assignment locking:

Right now, assignments are automatically locked once the due date passes, with no option to set a different close date. This prevents late submissions, putting us at a disadvantage compared to competitors who allow extended deadlines.

Confusing user interface

The current interface for scheduling and assigning tasks is not very intuitive, leading to teacher confusion. Due date and scheduled date fields lack clear context or explanations, making it hard for users to understand how these settings affect assignment management.

Limited control over assignment visibility

At the moment, assignments can either be scheduled for future release or published immediately. However, a program-level setting controls visibility for all scheduled assignments, meaning that if it‚Äôs enabled, everything becomes visible to students. Teachers can‚Äôt choose to show some tasks while hiding others, like assessments. Many competitors offer more flexible options, allowing teachers to publish or unpublish individual assignments, which better supports different teaching approaches.



User Goals


Our goal is to improve the task assignment process by making due dates, schedule dates, and close dates easier to manage and understand. We‚Äôll simplify the terms used and make it clearer for both students and teachers to track and handle assignments.
