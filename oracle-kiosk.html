Symphony Kiosk

Guest self-ordering within the restaurant.

Introduction

Oracle‚Äôs Food and Beverage Global Business Unit provides a comprehensive suite of enterprise software solutions designed for various restaurant types, including quick-service restaurants (QSRs), fast casuals, limited service restaurants, and stadiums. Our current system connects the Enterprise Management Console (EMC), Point of Sale (POS), and Kitchen Display System (KDS) to create a seamless food ordering experience. However, we‚Äôve identified a gap in the ordering process, particularly for QSRs and similar venues. To address this, we‚Äôre introducing a new entity‚Äîthe kiosk. This solution is aimed at increasing orders, influencing consumer buying behavior, and driving higher engagement.

Back Story

Oracle was building a new guest self-ordering experience with a kiosk, which deals with ordering the food to pick up the ordered food. The hardware was also built by Oracle FBGBU unit. Everything was to be built from scratch.

Currently

Identification of Problem

The restaurants and food courts at stadiums used to get so many orders daily. here is the list of identified problems ‚Äî
1. The staff to serve the orders was less.
2. Order entry errors.
3. The time to take orders was very high.
4. People with disabilities found it hard to give orders
5. The person who is introverted in nature was unable to order their food.
6. The delivery of ordered food items was a pain.
7. Making payments on receiving orders was a pain
8. Persons with language issues (mostly foreigners) are unable to order food efficiently.

How could building a Kiosk would reduce the amount of effort and solve the problems?

1. The effort to take orders will be drastically reduced as a result the less staff is required to take orders and they can focus on delivering the ordered food.
2. As the Person himself was ordering the food taking orders with errors was reduced.
3. With the kiosk, the ordering will be a very fast and smooth experience.
4. Persons with introverted nature can order on their own.
5. The delivery of food will be easy as the Kiosk gives an estimated time to get the food delivered and even gets a notification on mobile on food being ready to be delivered.
6. Using a kiosk, the payments are also made easy for the ordered food.
